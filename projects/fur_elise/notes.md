# Für Elise (opening)

**Status:** melody study / work in progress

The famous A-minor motif — 8 bars of 3/8 at 140 BPM (eighth-note pulse). Right-hand
melody with the iconic E5–D#5 alternation over a sustained bass (A2 under Am bars,
E2 under the leading-tone bar 4). Two tracks: Melody + Bass.

Currently only the A section (bars 1–8). The full piece has a B section (F major,
bars 23–37) and a C section (A major, bars 38–60) before returning to the A motif.

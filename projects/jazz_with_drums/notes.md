# Jazz With Drums

**Status:** sketch

ii–V–I in C major with a swing ride-cymbal pattern layered on top. Adds
a two-voice drum track (ride on every beat, swung "and" on 2 & 4, pedal hi-hat
on 2 & 4) to the basic chord progression.

## Possible next moves
- Add a walking bass (see `jazz_full`).
- Try a busier drum pattern: add kick, snare, ghost notes.
- Try a latin or bossa feel instead of swing.

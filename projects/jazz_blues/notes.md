# Jazz Blues

**Status:** sketch

F jazz blues — 12-bar form, 4 loops, walking bass, swing drums. Harmonic
highlights: the #IV diminished passing chord (Bdim7), the secondary dominant
(D7 → Gm7), and a ii–V turnaround (Gm7 → C7) at the end. The walking bass
approaches each root chromatically on the last beat.
